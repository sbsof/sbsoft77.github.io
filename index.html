Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Thank you for using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we manage and protect your privacy when you use this application.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, use, or share any personal information. This app functions entirely by processing the numbers and parameters you input locally on your device to perform calculations. None of the data you enter is ever transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. Device Permissions

This app may require certain device permissions solely to execute technical functions locally on your device (such as local storage to save your application preferences or previous calculation configurations). All such data remains securely on your own device.

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate or connect with any third-party services, analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Firebase), or external SDKs that could track your activities or collect data to identify you.

4. Security

We value your trust. Because this app does not request or transfer any personal data (such as names, emails, phone numbers, or location data), the risk of data leaks through this application is non-existent. However, please remember that no method of electronic storage or local device processing is 100% impenetrable.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us using the developer email address listed on the Google Play Store.
